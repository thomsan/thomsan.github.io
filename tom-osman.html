Techno DJ & Producer

Tom Osman (Thomas Ascioglu) is a Techno DJ & Producer living in Berlin.
During the lockdown in 2020 he converted his living room into a small club, where he improve his mixing skills and played for small groups.
He's still in the early days of his professional DJ career but has received great feedback at small spontaneous gigs around Berlin.
Tom Osman is actively looking for more opportunities to play in front of techno lovers.
