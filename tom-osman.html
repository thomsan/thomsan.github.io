Techno DJ & Producer

Tom Osman (Thomas Ascioglu) is a Techno DJ & Producer living in Berlin.
During the lockdown in 2020 Tom Osman started mixing and played for small groups. He's still in the early days of his DJ career but has received great feedback at different gigs around Berlin. Tom Osman is actively looking for more opportunities to play in front of techno lovers.

Tom Osman's music is influenced by Berlin's diverse techno scene, where he has been a regular guest at different clubs and events. As a DJ he likes to combine melodic with dark and hard elements to take his audience on a fast and energetic journey.
